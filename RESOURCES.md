# Acoustic Fingerstyle & Strumming — Resources

Curated, high-trust sources for this workspace. Knowledge in lessons should be
drawn from here, not from guesses.

## Knowledge

- [JustinGuitar — One Minute Changes Exercise](https://www.justinguitar.com/guitar-lessons/one-minute-changes-exercise-b1-110)
  The core drill for chord-change *speed*: time one minute, count how many clean
  changes between two chords you make; target ~60/min (one per second). Free,
  beginner-gold-standard. **Use for:** every new chord pair; the heart of the
  early foundation work.
- [JustinGuitar — Basic Travis Picking](https://www.justinguitar.com/guitar-lessons/basic-travis-picking-fo-108)
  Steady alternating-bass thumb under index/middle on the treble strings — the
  gateway pattern to all fingerstyle. **Use for:** the fingerpicking half of the
  mission, once chord changes are reliable.
- [Fender — Learn the Travis Picking Technique](https://www.fender.com/articles/techniques/travis-picking-on-guitar)
  Clear written explanation of the thumb/finger roles and syncopation.
  **Use for:** a second angle on Travis picking mechanics.
- [Guitar Noise — Basic Travis Finger Picking (Part 1](https://www.guitarnoise.com/lessons/basic-travis-finger-picking/) · [Part 2)](https://www.guitarnoise.com/lessons/basic-travis-finger-picking-part-2/)
  Patient, multi-part written tutorial with tabs; builds the pattern then adds
  pinches. **Use for:** step-by-step fingerpicking practice with notation.
- [JustinGuitar — Beginner Song lists](https://www.justinguitar.com/songs)
  Songs filtered by the chords/skills they require. **Use for:** picking the
  next song in the zone of proximal development (easy → advanced).

## Wisdom (Communities)

- [JustinGuitar Community](https://community.justinguitar.com/)
  Highly moderated, beginner-friendly forum tied to the lessons above.
  **Use for:** posting progress, getting technique feedback, staying motivated.
- [r/guitarlessons](https://www.reddit.com/r/guitarlessons/) and
  [r/acousticguitar](https://www.reddit.com/r/acousticguitar/)
  **Use for:** song suggestions, "is my hand position right" video critiques,
  troubleshooting plateaus. (Reddit — verify advice against the sources above.)
