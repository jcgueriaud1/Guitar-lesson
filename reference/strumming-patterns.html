Strumming Patterns

↓ = down-strum · ↑ = up-strum · faded = hand still swings but misses the strings. Count out loud: “1 & 2 & 3 & 4 &”.

The rule under every pattern: the hand never stops. Downs always fall on the numbers, ups on the “&”s — that's what keeps you in time. A pattern just chooses which of those swings actually hit the strings.
